International Courier Services

International Documents Express

 
For shipments that need to be delivered to other countries and do require customs clearance at either end, we recommend our Worldwide Parcel Express service. You may also need to ensure that certain documents accompany your parcels that are required by customs at the origin or destination of the parcel. Please note that certain duties and taxes may be levied by the relevant authorities when clearing the shipment through customs in the destination country. Furthermore, some delays may be experienced during this clearance process as determined by these authorities, however, we will endeavour to ensure that the process is as smooth as possible. We will provide you with an all inclusive price of collection, clearance and delivery (with the exception of customs duties and taxes). Our call centre agents are on standby to help you with understanding and performing these requirements. We may also help with INCO terms and Goods in Transit insurance on consultation.

Worldwilde Parcel Express

 
For those shipments that must be there first thing in the morning. The shipments will be collected the day prior to delivery, which will happen between 07h00 and 09h00 in the city centres. Please contact our call centre on to consult with one of our call centre agents who are there to help.
 

Import Express

 
This is a flexible importation solution that allows you to be in control of your imports of documents and parcels. We can provide you with a fixed determinable cost in South African Rands that like the Worldwide Parcel Express, includes collection, clearance and delivery (with the exception of customs duties and taxes). Please consult with our call centre agents who are waiting to make your importation simple and effective.
 

Fuel Surcharge

 

Please note that all tariffs are subject to a fluctuating fuel surcharge due to the extreme volatility of the Oil price, and subsequently jet fuel. This is a percentage surcharge calculated from an index to the base tariff, which is based on the price of fuel one month in arrears, for example, December's fuel surcharge will be based on the average fuel price for October. Whilst we make every attempt to minimise the impact, this is an industry norm and is passed from airlines to carriers to customers. The index is based on an industry standard, being the Rotterdam ARA Index which we welcome our customers to view.

Freight Solutions

Roadfreight

 
For those heavier and consolidated shipments within the borders of South Africa that you can afford to have delivered within 3 to 4 days, we recommend our economical Deferred National Roadfreight service. We can also offer a Cross-Border Roadfreight service to neighbouring countries on request. We'll need a little notice to effect the collection and transportation, and our call centre agents are on standby to help you through the process.

Airfreight

 
For international or cross-border shipments that are heavier and less time sensitive, we recommend using our Airfreight service. Through relationships with key airfreight forwarders, Avion Express is able to draw on infrastructure, expertise with superior systems at a highly competitive rate.

Seafreight

 
For larger shipments that can afford longer lead times, stretching into weeks, we recommend our Seafreight service. This is a more cost effective solution for larger bulk and consolidated shipments. We draw on the capabilities and experience of our partners to give a competitive edge to our customers.
 

Fuel Surcharge

 

Please note that all tariffs are subject to a fluctuating fuel surcharge due to the extreme volatility of the Oil price, and subsequently jet fuel. This is a percentage surcharge calculated from an index to the base tariff, which is based on the price of fuel one month in arrears, for example, December's fuel surcharge will be based on the average fuel price for October. Whilst we make every attempt to minimise the impact, this is an industry norm and is passed from airlines to carriers to customers. The index is based on an industry standard, being the Rotterdam ARA Index which we welcome our customers to view.

Company Profile
 

Avion Express is a Level 1 BEE logistics fulfillment business who through our service partners offer an unrivalled portfolio of logistics services ranging from national and international parcel delivery, e-commerce shipping and fulfillment solutions, international express, road, air and ocean transport to industrial supply chain management.

Avion Express connects people and businesses securely and reliably, enabling global trade flows. With specialized solutions for growth markets and industries including technology, life sciences and healthcare, energy, automotive, printing and retail, a proven commitment to corporate responsibility and an unrivalled presence in developing markets, Avion Express is decisively positioned as “The logistics company for South Africa”.

We offer a competitive, reliable fast Domestic and International courier solution. Our international network allows for delivery to over 220 countries, as well as in excess of 3000 domestic destinations. We provide personal, professional and friendly service through our hands on approach. With over 20 years of courier knowledge and experience it allows us to find the perfect courier solution for you. Whether you’re sending an urgent international parcel, a time-critical domestic courier or an economy Road freight shipment, we will offer World-class global logistics services with professional courier support from our expert customer service team.

At Avion Express Information availability is critical to the success of our business and we pride ourself in our ability to track all internal and external processes. State of the art security systems and tracking devices are utilized to minimize risk along the logistics channel and ensure that movement of product is tracked and monitored at all times.
